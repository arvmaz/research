# AI VRP Research

## What is the AI VRP?

Google's AI Vulnerability Reward Program, launched 2024/2025. Covers:
- Gemini
- Vertex AI  
- AI products built on Google infrastructure

## Can Hermes participate?

**Yes.** Hermes can:
- Research AI-specific vulnerability classes (prompt injection, task injection, data exfiltration)
- Find vulnerabilities in AI product APIs
- Explore attack surfaces in Gemini / Vertex AI

## Notes from existing research

- Blog post: "Announcing Google's New AI Vulnerability Reward Program!" (Oct 2025) — check bughunters for latest scope
- Less mature than Chrome/Android VRP → potentially less competition
- Reward amounts vary — not publicly standardized like main VRP

## Priority for Hermes

**Medium.** AI VRP is newer, less documented, but growing rapidly.
